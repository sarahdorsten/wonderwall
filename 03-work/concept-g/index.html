What this is

Crossroads runs a broadcast-grade virtual production environment in front of a live congregation every week. Real-time Unreal Engine. GhostFrame on three camera slices. Eight RED Komodo cameras. Full L-ISA spatial audio from a 3,400-seat room.

No pre-render.
No second take.

The stage transforms completely for every event — Christmas, Super Bowl, Easter, an immersive experience called Real Encounters Journey. Same physical structure. Radically different worlds, built and switched live.

"These systems allow a small weekly crew to run a production comparable to major touring or broadcast events."

— Craig Dockery, Creative Director, Crossroads Church
