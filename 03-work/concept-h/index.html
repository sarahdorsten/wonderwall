What this is

The Wonderwall is nine independent LED surfaces — four that physically move and reconfigure mid-service. It runs Unreal Engine environments live, no pre-renders, in front of 3,400 people. GhostFrame embeds separate signals into the LED refresh cycle so what cameras see is different from what the room sees, simultaneously. The same crew resets it for a completely different production every week.

World First — 2025

In-person and broadcast audiences see two different versions of the same stage. At the same time.

GhostFrame embeds multiple video signals into the LED refresh cycle simultaneously. In-person attendees see one environment. Broadcast cameras capture something else entirely from the same surface — no switching, no tricks, both live at once, in front of 3,400 people.

"The implementation at Crossroads Oakley marks the largest known use of GhostFrame technology in a live venue with an in-person audience." Scott Lau — Technical Project Manager, Disguise
