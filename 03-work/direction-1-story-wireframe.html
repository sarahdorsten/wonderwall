Wonderwall

Create at the edge of
technology and meaning

Church has historically been a driving force behind art, music and expression. And we believe it should be today.

Join us on an ambitious journey as we explore the possibilities of storytelling in the digital age. Think The Vegas Sphere, but built to tell stories that change lives.

Immersive Environments

No two weekends are the same. The Wonderwall transforms the room into a responsive visual canvas where story, light, music, architecture, and motion work together to create experiences people can step inside.

Live Production

We operate at the intersection of live performance, cinematic storytelling, and technical precision. A lean team executes at a level more commonly associated with arena tours and high-end broadcast environments.

Broadcast XR

Blend the physical stage with real-time virtual production to create experiences for the room and the camera. Using Unreal Engine, GhostFrame, RED workflows, Disguise, and advanced media systems, we are pushing the limits every week.

Built with

Live Production Infrastructure

A 3,400-seat room powered by broadcast, XR, tracking, audio, automation, LED, and real-time rendering systems.

Room + audience

  • Approx. 3,400 seats
  • 1,700 floor / 900 balcony 1 / 800 balcony 2
  • Live congregation, performers, cues, and cameras

Broadcast core

  • Full UHD HDR video core
  • Ross Ultrix 12RU core router
  • Ross Acuity switchers
  • Barco E3 screen control

Cameras

  • RED KOMODO-X 6K cameras
  • S35 Global Shutter camera system
  • Integrated with Megapixel for GhostFrame

Real-time + media servers

  • Disguise Gx3+ / Gx3 media servers
  • Disguise Rx3 render nodes
  • Silverdraft Demon Workstations
  • Unreal Engine, Notch, TouchDesigner

XR / AR + tracking

  • Set extension through Disguise / D3
  • GhostFrame with 3 slices
  • Stype RedSpy camera tracking
  • Stage Precision Grid with TiMax talent tracking

Stage systems

  • ROE BP2v2, ROE Vanish, ROE BM4, ROE Jasper
  • Megapixel HELIOS LED processing
  • TAIT motion control

Audio system

  • DiGiCo Quantum7 FOH / Quantum5 monitors
  • L-Acoustics L2 L-ISA
  • Immersive audio integration

Lighting rig

  • Robe, Martin, and Chauvet lighting
  • Robe iSpiider / Robe Forte FS
  • Martin Mac One / Chauvet Pxl Bar 16
