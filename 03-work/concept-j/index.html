What this is

A game ships once. A film wraps. Broadcast runs its cycle and moves on. None of those put you in front of a live audience every seven days with a complete reset.

The Wonderwall runs Unreal Engine environments live — no pre-renders — while the stage physically reconfigures around it. A small crew. The same people. Every week.

The Stack

Rendering

Unreal Engine

Real-time via RenderStream. Navigate the 3D environment during the service — not before it. Not after it.

Disguise

29 servers. Gx3+ (7x) · Gx3 (10x) · Rx3 (12x).

Notch

Real-time effects composited with UE environments and live feeds. No pre-renders anywhere in the chain.

Display

ROE LED — 9 Surfaces

BP2v2, BM4, Jasper 2.6, Vanish. Four surfaces are automated moving walls. The stage reconfigures between environments.

Megapixel HELIOS

LED processing · GhostFrame integration. Two signals, one refresh cycle, zero switching.

Camera & Tracking

8x RED KOMODO-X

GhostFrame-integrated. Each camera tracked in 3D via Stype. Frame-accurate frustum alignment — the AR layer locks to the lens, not the room.

Stage Precision · TiMax

Spatial calibration and talent tracking. Every element knows exactly where it is in 3D space.

Signal & Automation

Ross Video Ultrix FR12

4K routing · 12 Gbps pipeline. Broadcast-grade signal chain — same infrastructure as major live events.

TAIT Automation

LED wall rigging. The walls move mid-service. Not between services. During.

Audio

L-Acoustics L-ISA

Object-based spatial audio. Sound position moves with environment changes.

DiGiCo Quantum7 · Quantum5

FOH and monitors. Same consoles on major touring productions.
